About Me

I'm Aadithya Srinivasan, a freshman at the University of Pennsylvania studying Computer Science, with a passion for using technology to drive social impact. I founded EcoHarmony, a global platform promoting recycling awareness, and co-founded IvyRise, a college consulting company helping students navigate the admissions process. My technical experience includes web development, AI/ML, and cybersecurity, with projects ranging from client persona generation to neural networks for skin cancer classification. I’ve also led educational initiatives for middle schoolers and underserved communities, teaching math and computer science. I’m committed to creating sustainable tech-driven solutions that address societal challenges, blending innovation with a desire to help others.
