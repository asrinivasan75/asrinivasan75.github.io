About Me

I'm a student at the University of Pennsylvania pursuing a B.S.E. in Computer Science and Mathematics with coursework in Machine Learning, Algorithms, Big Data Analytics, and Human–Computer Interaction. I'm passionate about building scalable systems and intelligent models at the intersection of software engineering, data analytics, and quantitative research.

I've interned as a Data Science Intern at Nayya, where I engineered SQL + Python pipelines processing over 100 million medical and pharmacy claims, built anomaly detection systems, and delivered analytics dashboards for cost optimization and pricing strategy. Previously, at Aristotle (Your AI SDR), I developed AI-driven backend services using AWS, TypeScript, and Python.

Beyond internships, I enjoy designing and deploying advanced AI/ML projects — such as a UCI-compatible Chess Engine with PyTorch-based evaluation, a Reinforcement Learning PokerBot, and a full-stack Supplement Recommender SaaS. My technical work blends algorithmic depth with end-to-end system design.

Outside of academics, I'm active in Penn's quantitative and mathematical communities, including the Wharton Investment & Trading Group, Penn Poker Club, and Penn Undergraduate Mathematics Society. I also compete in chess (2300 rating, USCF 99th percentile) and enjoy strategy games and probabilistic modeling.
